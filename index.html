Welcome to West Shore Physiotherapy

West Shore Physiotherapy and Sports Medicine is a multi-disciplinary clinic which strives to provide a superior physiotherapy experience to Innisfil residents. Our main focus is to ensure our patients are educated about their injury and to help guide them in an efficient recovery. We ensure your experience with us is patient focused, from when you walk in the Clinic until your last treatment. We are proud to serve the Innisfil community and look forward to your call.
